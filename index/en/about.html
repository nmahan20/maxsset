About.

MAXSSET is focused on design, inspect and investigation on the assets with dedicated team of qualified and experienced engineers. Our Values.

At MAXSSET we remain focused on our fundamental principles while growing towards global platforms. This ensures us that the requirements of our Clients are met in a timely and cost-effective manner. We serve our Clients based on our PRECISE principals:

People: We take pride in talent of our people as they are the reason behind our success.
Respect: We believe in professional relationships built on mutual respect.
Excellence: We endeavour for highest quality and excellence in all aspect of our works.
Creativity: We value innovation to drive the project costs down.
Integrity: We value honesty and upholding ethical behaviour.
Safety: We never compromise on safety.
Efficiency: We maintain our competitiveness by improving our services.

Quality Policy.

We are committed to providing quality services to our clients and this policy outlines our ongoing obligations to our clients in respect of how we manage their Personal Information.

We have adopted the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) contained in the Privacy Act 1988 (the Privacy Act). The APPs govern the way in which we collect, use, disclose, store, secure and dispose of our clients Personal Information.

A copy of the Australian Privacy Principles may be obtained from the website of The Office of the Australian Information Commissioner at www.aoic.gov.au
